When Will They Meet?

Overview of Challenge

This challenge is to see when the most likely time is that two agents will meet each other.

Challenge Specifics

Agents live on a 2-D infinite grid and every second, they randomly move up, down, left, or right with equal probability. Their first step is at second 0. They begin with 40 spaces between each other. What is the most likely second that they will meet each other? This answer is the key to the encrypted message. See the repository https://github.com/prestonbrubaker/text_file_encryption/tree/main for a program that can be used to decode the encrypted message using the most likely second of meeting as the key.
